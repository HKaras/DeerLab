correctzerotime¶

deerlab.correctzerotime(V, t)[source]¶

Determines and corrects the zero-time of a dipolar signal via a first-moment integral analysis [1].

Parameters:
V : array_like

Dipolar signal.

t : array_like

Time axis, in microseconds.
Returns:
tcorr : ndarray

Zero-time corrected time axis, in microseconds.

References

[1]G. Jeschke, V. Chechik, P. Ionita, A. Godt, H. Zimmermann, J. Banham, C. R. Timmel, D. Hilger and H. Jung, DeerAnalysis2006—a comprehensive software package for analyzing pulsed ELDOR data Applied Magnetic Resonance 30, 2006, 473-498
